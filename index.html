Common Questions

Vaccine FAQs

Do I need an appointment?

Tetanus (Tdap/Td) shots are walk-in — no appointment needed. Hepatitis B and Shingles vaccines require calling ahead so we can confirm stock and schedule your visit.

I have a cut or puncture wound — do I need a tetanus shot?

If it's been more than 5 years since your last tetanus shot and you have a deep or dirty wound, a booster is often recommended. Walk in any time we're open — no appointment necessary. For serious wounds, seek emergency care first.

How many doses of the Shingles vaccine do I need?

Shingrix is given as a 2-dose series, typically 2 to 6 months apart. Call ahead to schedule both doses.

Who needs the Hepatitis B vaccine?

Hepatitis B vaccination is often recommended for healthcare workers, travelers to certain countries, and anyone with increased exposure risk. Call us and we can help you figure out if it's right for you.

How much do these vaccines cost?

Most major insurance plans — including Medicare Part B, Medicare Part D, Medi-Cal, and commercial plans — cover recommended vaccines at little to no cost. If you're uninsured, a cash price is available; ask our pharmacist.

Where is the clinic located?
