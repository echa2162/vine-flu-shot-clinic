Common Questions

Flu shot FAQs.

01Do I need an appointment for a flu shot?

No. Flu shots are walk-in at Vine Flu Shot Clinic — just stop by Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM. No appointment necessary.

02How much does a flu shot cost?

Most major insurance plans — including Medicare Part B, Medicare Part D, Medi-Cal, and commercial plans — cover your flu shot at $0 cost. If you're uninsured, a cash price is available; ask our pharmacist.

03How long does it take?

Most visits take just a few minutes from check-in to vaccination. There's rarely a wait.

04Who administers the flu shot?

Our licensed pharmacist, Roseanne Papoyan, PharmD, administers vaccinations on-site.

05Where is the clinic located?

We're at 1253 N Vine St, Suite 11, Los Angeles, CA 90038 — the corner of Vine & Fountain, steps from Capitol Records and the Hollywood Pantages Theatre.

06What should I bring?

Bring a photo ID and your insurance card if you have one. We'll verify your coverage before your shot.
